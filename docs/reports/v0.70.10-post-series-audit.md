## 5. Risks & Trade-offs Accepted

1. **Typed Racket stopped at 1 module** ÔÇö v0.70.9 pilot succeeded but no further conversions to avoid facade churn. Future work should continue with leaf utilities.
2. **Shell risk classifier shadow-mode** ÔÇö structured classifier runs alongside regex but does not enforce. Promotion to enforcement requires user confidence threshold.
3. **macOS/Windows credential backends** ÔÇö added but not enabled by default. Burn-in period recommended before making them default fallbacks.
4. **Test decomposition incomplete** ÔÇö `test-cli.rkt` and `test-gemini.rkt` decomposed, but other large files remain (`tests/interfaces/tui.rkt` at 1123 lines, `tests/test-golden-flows.rkt` at 1069 lines).
